{Leisl's Products for Sale}

Many of Leisl's design and art works are available not only as wall-worthy art prints, but on a variety of everyday consumer products on sale at print-on-demand retail platforms.

Please note that not all designs are available on all products, or on all platforms (i.e., in all tiers of quality versus affordability). Contact me if you need help finding the right product or gift, or to request a new product offering.
